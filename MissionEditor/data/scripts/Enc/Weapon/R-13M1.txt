Name: R-13M1 (AA-2 'Advanced Atoll D')
Type: short-range, infrared-guided, air-to-air 
missile
Developed: Russia
Warhead weight, kg: 11.3
Guidance: infrared
Weight, kg: 90.6
Maximum Mach number: 2.5
Length, m: 2.876
Body diameter, m: 0.127
Range, km: 15

The R-13M1 was an improved version of the R-13M missile with modified seeker "INEY-M" and new cranked canards provided better maneuverability. The missile entered service in the middle of 70's.
