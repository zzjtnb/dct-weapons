Name: S-8KOM
Type: 80 mm unguided aircraft rocket
Developed: Spetztekhnika Vympel NPO, Russia
Weight, kg: 11.3
Length, m: 1.57
Caliber, mm: 80
Warhead weight, kg: 3.6
Warhead type: shaped-charge fragmentation
Speed, m/s: 610
Firing range, m: 1300 - 4000
Armor penetration, mm: 400

This rocket is intended to engage modern tanks, lightly armored and soft-skinned combat materiel. Owing to the fragmentation effect, the rocket also inflicts damage on manpower.
