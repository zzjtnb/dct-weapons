Name: SC-50
Developed: 1933
Type: General Purpose Bomb
Country of Origin: Germany
Weight, kg: 50
Length, m: 1.095
Body diameter, m: 0.2
Explosive Charge, kg: 23, 40/60 Amatol-TNT (Trialen in the anti-ship variant)

SC series bombs were general purpose bombs of different sizes with nominal weights from 50 to 2500 kg.
