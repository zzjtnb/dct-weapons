Name: Mk 82 Snake Eye
Type: General purpose bomb
Developed: USA
Weight, kg: 258
Length, m: 2.27
Body diameter, m: 0.274
Warhead weight, kg: 136

The Mk 82 Snake Eye bomb is a general purpose bomb with a fin retarding device. The high drag caused by opened fins permits high-speed, low-angle and/or low-altitude delivery by eliminating the danger of aircraft damage due to ricocheting bombs or fragments from detonating bombs.
