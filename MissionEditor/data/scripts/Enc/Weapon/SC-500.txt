Name: SC-500
Developed: 1935
Type: General Purpose Bomb
Country of Origin: Germany
Weight, kg: 500
Length, m: 2.022
Body diameter, m: 0.47
Explosive Charge, kg: 270, 40/60 Amatol-TNT (Trialen in the anti-ship variant)
Fuze Type: a variety of fuze types including non-delay and delayed action fuzes.

SC series bombs were general purpose bombs of different sizes with nominal weights from 50 to 2500 kg.
