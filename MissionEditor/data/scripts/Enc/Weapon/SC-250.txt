Name: SC-250
Developed: 1933
Type: General Purpose Bomb
Country of Origin: Germany
Weight, kg: 250
Length, m: 1.64
Body diameter, m: 0.37
Explosive Charge, kg: 135, 40/60 Amatol-TNT (Trialen in the anti-ship variant)
Fuze Type: a variety of fuze types including non-delay and delayed action fuzes.

SC-250 (Sprengbombe Cylindrisch 250) - was an air-dropped general purpose bomb built by Germany during World War II and used intensively during the whole period. It could be carried by almost all German bomber aircraft. The bomb's weight was about 250 kg.
