The 2B11 is a 120 mm mortar developed by the Soviet Union in 1979. The mortar is a part of the 2S12 "Sani" heavy mortar system that includes a 2B11 mortar itself, the 2F510 or the 2F510A transport vehicle, the 2L81 2x1 wheeled chassis and the spares and tool kit.
